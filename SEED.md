# Seed: The Forgotten Workshop

Public repositories contain thousands of abandoned tools, prototypes, experiments, lists, games, scripts, datasets, and strange utilities.

Some failed because they were bad. Some failed because they were early. Some were useful but never finished. Some are just interesting fragments.

Explore this world.

Find patterns, absences, neglected ideas, or reusable shapes.

Then turn what you learn into a working artifact.

The final project may be practical, creative, analytical, educational, playful, or strange — but it must become concrete.

The seed is not a topic assignment. It is a starting pressure. You may move beyond it, but every departure must be justified in `DECISIONS.md`.
